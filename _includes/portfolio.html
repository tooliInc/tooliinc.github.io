Rent out your tools

  • 1

    Create an account

    Simply login through Google or e-mail to get started. Setup a profile so we know how to get money to you.
  • 2

    List your tools

    List all your tools that you’d like to rent. Provide the required details and use the app to take a picture of your tool. Rent it out for the price you want.
  • 3

    Manage bookings

    If you need the tool for yourself or it’s not available, simply mark on the calendar when it’s not available. Have all your bookings in an easy to update format.
  • 4

    Review the borrower

    Your tools have been treated with love. Make sure the person renting them does the same by reviewing the borrower. Just to make sure though, your tools are also fully insured.

Borrow any tool

  • 1

    Select the category

    Pick the appropriate category for the type of project you’re working on.
  • 2

    Select the tool

    Find the right tool, at the right price at the right location convenient for you..
  • 3

    Read details and reviews

    Read the details of the tool to make sure it’s appropriate for what you need. Read past reviews to ensure other have had a good experience with that exact tool.
  • 4

    Book the tool

    Contact the tool owner with any other questions and confirm a pickup location for the tool. Book the tool and it’s ready to be used.
